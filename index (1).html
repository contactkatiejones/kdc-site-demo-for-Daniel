What We Offer

Our Services

Full-service repair and maintenance for semi trucks, commercial vehicles, buses, and trailers — all under one roof in Indianapolis.

🔧

Preventive Maintenance & Oil Changes

Scheduled PM services keep your trucks compliant and your downtime minimal. Oil changes, fluid top-offs, filter replacements, belts, hoses, and full chassis lube. Fleet service options available — we'll build a PM schedule around your operation.

📋

DOT Inspections

Annual DOT inspections performed by certified technicians. Thorough Level 1 inspections with detailed reports. Pre-trip inspection support also available. Fleet inspection packages offered. Get back on the road compliant and confident.

💥

Full Wreck Repairs

Complete collision and accident repair for semi trucks, commercial vehicles, and trailers. Frame straightening, structural repair, cab restoration, and full rebuild — we handle it from start to finish and get your equipment back earning.

🎨

Paint — Two 60' Cross Draft Paint Booths

Professional-grade painting in our two 60-foot cross draft paint booths — large enough for full semi trucks and trailers. Complete body prep, primer, color matching, and topcoat finish. Fleet repaints and single units welcome.

💨

Sandblasting

Industrial sandblasting for trucks, trailers, frames, and components. Removes rust, old paint, and corrosion down to bare metal — ideal prep for painting, welding, or protective coatings.

🚛

Mobile Service — 3 Equipped Service Trucks

Can't bring your truck in? We come to you. Three fully equipped service trucks dispatched for on-site repairs and maintenance for both trucks and trailers. Ideal for fleet accounts, breakdowns, and yard service.

📝

On-Site Estimates

We provide estimates right at your location — no need to bring the vehicle in just to find out what it'll cost. Straightforward, written estimates with no surprises.

💻

J-Pro Computer Diagnostic System

Our J-Pro computer system provides advanced diagnostics for commercial trucks and equipment. Accurate fault identification means faster, more precise repairs — less guesswork, less downtime.

🛑

Brakes & Suspension

Complete brake system service including air brakes, hydraulic brakes, drum and disc replacements, slack adjusters, and brake chambers. Full suspension diagnostics, leaf springs, shocks, and alignment checks.

🚜

Trailer Repair

All trailer types — dry van, flatbed, refrigerated, tanker, and lowboy. Structural repairs, flooring, lighting systems, rear doors, landing gear, kingpin service, and undercarriage work.

⚫

Tires

Commercial tire sales, mounting, balancing, and rotation. Wide selection of steer, drive, and trailer tires from top brands. Flat repair and mobile tire service available.

🔌

Electrical & Lighting

Diagnosis and repair of truck and trailer electrical systems. Running lights, brake lights, ABS systems, wiring harnesses, and battery service. DOT lighting compliance handled.

KDC Truck & Trailer Repair has been serving Indianapolis since 1995 — built on a simple idea: commercial drivers and fleet operators deserve a shop they can actually trust. One that gives them straight answers, fair prices, and repairs done right the first time.

We're a local, independent Indianapolis shop. Over 30 years we've grown our capabilities to include full wreck repairs, two 60' cross draft paint booths, sandblasting, and three fully equipped mobile service trucks. Most shops in Indianapolis have to send that work out. We do it all in-house.

When your truck is down, your income is down — and we take that seriously. Every job gets a check-in text, a diagnosis update before we start, and a ready notification when it's done. No surprises, no chasing us for updates.
